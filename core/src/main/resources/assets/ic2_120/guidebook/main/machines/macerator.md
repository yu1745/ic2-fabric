# Macerator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:macerator" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Macerator grinds ores and other materials into dust. It is primarily used for ore doubling — each ore block yields two dust, which can then be smelted into two ingots. It also processes various other materials into their dust forms.

## Output

- **Tier:** 1
- **Energy Usage:** ~20 EU/t per operation

## Slots

- Top left slot: input
- Top right slot: output
