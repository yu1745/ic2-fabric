# Solid Heat Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:solid_heat_generator" scale="4" />

The Solid Heat Generator burns furnace fuels to produce heat (HU). It is the heat equivalent of the Generator — instead of producing electricity, it generates heat for use in heat-based machines and processes.

## Output

The Solid Heat Generator produces **10 HU/t** while burning fuel. Its internal heat buffer stores up to **1,000 HU**.

Fuel burn time is based on the vanilla furnace fuel value divided by 4, matching the Generator's behavior but producing heat instead of electricity.

## Slots

- Top slot: fuel input (coal, charcoal, planks, etc.)
- Bottom slot: (not used)

## Usage

Heat is output from all faces and can be routed through heat-conducting blocks or directly into adjacent heat machines such as the Liquid Heat Exchanger.
