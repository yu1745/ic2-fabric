# Iron Rotor

<ItemImage id="ic2_120:iron_rotor" scale="4" />

The Iron Rotor is a mid-tier rotor used in Wind Kinetic Generators and Water Kinetic Generators.

- **Wind**: Radius 3, Multiplier 2&times;
- **Water**: Radius 1.5, Multiplier 2&times;

It offers improved performance over the wooden rotor, with a larger radius and doubled power multiplier for both wind and water applications.
