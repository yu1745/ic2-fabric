# Batteries and Mobile Power

Battery items can be charged and discharged in energy storage blocks, charging pads, and some machines. Higher tiers bring larger capacity and faster charge/discharge rates.

## Hand-Held Batteries

| Item | Tier | Capacity | Rate | Notes |
|------|:---:|---:|---:|------|
| Re-Battery | 1 | 10,000 EU | 32 EU/t | The most basic rechargeable battery |
| Advanced Re-Battery | 2 | 100,000 EU | 128 EU/t | Mid-game tool battery |
| Energy Crystal | 3 | 1,000,000 EU | 512 EU/t | Right-click to recharge powered items in your inventory |
| Lapotron Crystal | 4 | 10,000,000 EU | 2048 EU/t | High-capacity portable power |
| Single-Use Battery | 1 | 10,000 EU | 32 EU/t | Discharges only; disappears when empty |

## Wireless Batteries

Wireless batteries have 4× the capacity of their base counterpart and act as automatic top-up sources.

| Item | Tier | Capacity |
|------|:---:|---:|
| Wireless Re-Battery | 1 | 40,000 EU |
| Advanced Wireless Re-Battery | 2 | 400,000 EU |
| Wireless Energy Crystal | 3 | 4,000,000 EU |
| Wireless Lapotron Crystal | 4 | 40,000,000 EU |

## Battery Packs

Battery Packs are worn in the chestplate slot and automatically recharge any powered tool in your inventory whose tier is at or below the pack's tier.

| Pack | Tier | Capacity |
|------|:---:|---:|
| BatPack | 1 | 60,000 EU |
| Advanced BatPack | 2 | 600,000 EU |
| Energy Pack | 3 | 2,000,000 EU |
| LapPack | 4 | 60,000,000 EU |

## Energy Storage Blocks

- BatBox, CESU, MFE, and MFSU are stationary energy storage blocks.
- The Charging Pad variant recharges a player's gear and items while they stand on top.
- See [Energy Storage](../machines/energy_storage.md) and [Charging Pad](../machines/chargepad.md) for details.
