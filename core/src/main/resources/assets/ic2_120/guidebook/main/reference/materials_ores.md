# Materials and Ores

## World Resources

- **Ores**: tin, lead, uranium, peat, plus the corresponding deepslate variants.
- **Rare resources**: iridium ore, iridium shards, reinforced iridium plate — key late-game materials for energy storage and nuclear components.
- **Rubber**: rubber trees provide sticky resin, which can be processed into rubber.

## Metals and Base Materials

| Material | Common Forms | Use |
|------|----------|------|
| Copper | dust, plate, casing, cable | Early circuits, heat exchange, cables |
| Tin | ingot, dust, plate, casing, cell | Batteries, cells, cooling components |
| Bronze | ingot, dust, plate, casing, tools | Early tools, tanks, pipes |
| Lead | ingot, dust, plate, casing | Nuclear, shielding, some batteries |
| Silver | ingot, dust | Glass fibre cable and other advanced recipes |
| Steel | ingot, plate, casing | Reinforced structures, tools, machines |
| Uranium | ore, ingot, dust, fuel | Nuclear fuel chain |

## Processing Chain

- Raw ore can be fed into the Macerator, Ore Washing Plant, Thermal Centrifuge chain to improve yield or extract by-products.
- Crushed ore can be washed further into Purified Crushed Ore.
- Dusts, ingots, plates, casings, and dense plates are the intermediate items used by most machines and tools.
- The Forge Hammer turns ingots into plates, and plates into casings.
- The Plate Cutting Shears cut plates into cables, and strip insulation off cables.

## Carbon and Advanced Materials

- Coal dust, coal balls, compressed hydrated coal, and coal chunks feed into the carbon material chain.
- Crude carbon fibre, crude carbon mesh, and carbon plate are used in advanced tools, armor, and rotors.
- Advanced alloy, industrial diamond, energium dust, and iridium plate are used in late-game machines and gear.
