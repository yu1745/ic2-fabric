# Thermal Centrifuge

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:centrifuge" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Thermal Centrifuge processes purified washed ores or other materials by spinning them at high speed, separating them into multiple refined outputs. It is the third step in the IC2 ore processing chain, after the Ore Washing Plant.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 10,000 EU
- **Tier:** 2
- **Energy Usage:** ~50 EU/t per operation

## Slots

- Left slot: input (purified crushed ore)
- Right slots: multiple outputs depending on material
