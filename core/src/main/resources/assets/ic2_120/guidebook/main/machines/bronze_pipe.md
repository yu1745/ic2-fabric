# Bronze Pipe

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:bronze_pipe_tiny" scale="4" />

Bronze pipes transport fluids between tanks and machines. They come in four sizes with different throughput capacities. Bronze pipes connect automatically to adjacent tanks, machines, and other pipes.

## Size Comparison

| Size | Throughput | Use Case |
|------|------------|----------|
| Tiny | 0.5 B/t (500 mB/t max) | Low-volume precision transfer |
| Small | 1 B/t (1,000 mB/t max) | General purpose |
| Medium | 2 B/t (2,000 mB/t max) | High-volume transfer |
| Large | 4 B/t (4,000 mB/t max) | Bulk fluid transport |

## Usage

- Pipes connect to adjacent fluid tanks, machines, and other pipes automatically.
- Fluids flow through connected pipes from a source (tank, pump) to a destination.
- A Pump Attachment is required to extract fluids from tanks and machines automatically.
- Right-click the pipe with a wrench to configure connection sides.
- Pipes retain their contents when broken.
