# RTG Heat Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:rt_heat_generator" scale="4" />

The Radioisotope Thermoelectric (RTG) Heat Generator produces heat (HU) constantly from radioactive isotopes. Unlike other heat generators, it requires no fuel input and no EU — it generates heat passively over time as long as it is loaded with radioactive material.

## Output

The RTG Heat Generator produces heat continuously without consuming fuel. The amount of heat generated depends on the radioactive isotopes installed:

- **Fuel Rod (Uranium)**: generates a small amount of heat continuously
- **RTG Pellet**: generates more heat per pellet

## Slots

- Top slot: radioactive isotope fuel (fuel rods, RTG pellets)
- Bottom slot: depleted fuel output

## Usage

Insert RTG pellets or fuel rods into the top slot. The generator will produce heat (HU) indefinitely at a steady rate until the fuel is depleted. Depleted fuel will appear in the bottom slot. Heat is output from all faces.

The RTG Heat Generator is ideal for remote or long-running installations where refueling is impractical.
