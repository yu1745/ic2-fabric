# Recall Scroll

## Purpose

A Recall Scroll can be bound to a Teleporter, letting you teleport directly back to that Teleporter from anywhere in the field.

## How to Use

1. **Bind**: hold the Recall Scroll and right-click the Teleporter to bind it
2. **Teleport**: hold a bound Recall Scroll and right-click to teleport back to the bound Teleporter

## Notes

- **Same dimension**: only teleports within the same dimension; cross-dimension teleports are not allowed
- **Energy cost**: teleportation drains power from batteries in your inventory (Re-Battery, Energy Crystal, etc.). Cost scales with distance and the number of items you are carrying
- **Bound appearance**: once bound, the scroll's icon changes from a blank map to a filled map
- **Anchor destroyed**: if the bound Teleporter is broken, the teleport still goes through, but you take a harsh penalty
- Each player can only hold one Recall Scroll at a time
