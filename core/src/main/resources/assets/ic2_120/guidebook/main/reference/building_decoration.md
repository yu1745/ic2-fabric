# Building and Decoration

## Rubber Wood

Rubber Trees provide a full set of wood blocks: Rubber Log, Rubber Wood, Stripped Rubber Log, Stripped Rubber Wood, Rubber Planks, stairs, slabs, doors, trapdoors, buttons, pressure plates, fences, fence gates, and signs.

## Reinforced Building

- Reinforced Stone, Reinforced Glass, and Reinforced Door are ideal for protecting high-value machines and nuclear areas.
- Iron Fence works well as industrial-style fencing and barriers.
- Reactor Pressure Vessels are nuclear-structure blocks, see [Nuclear Blocks and Components](nuclear_components.md).

## Construction Foam

- Construction Foam can be applied via Construction Foam buckets, cells, the Sprayer, or machine chains.
- Reinforced Construction Foam is for sturdier structures.
- Foam Walls come in 16 dyed variants: white, orange, magenta, light blue, yellow, lime, pink, gray, light gray, cyan, purple, blue, brown, green, red, black.
- Resin Sheets, Rubber Sheets, and Wool Sheets are thin decorative / functional blocks.

## Scaffolding

- Wooden Scaffold and Iron Scaffold are for temporary construction work.
- Reinforced Wooden Scaffold and Reinforced Iron Scaffold are better suited for long-term structures.
- Mining Pipes belong to the [Miner](../machines/miner.md) working chain, not the regular fluid pipes.

## Miscellaneous Items

- <ItemLink id="ic2_120:itnt" /> (Industrial TNT) — A much more powerful explosive than vanilla TNT. It has a significantly higher blast radius and deals more damage, making it useful for large-scale demolition and mining. Handle with care.
- <ItemLink id="ic2_120:coin" /> (Industrial Coin) — Industrial Currency crafted from iron casings via the <ItemLink id="ic2_120:metal_former" /> (Extruding mode). Coins are used in certain IC2 recipes.
