# Solar Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:solar_generator" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Solar Generator converts sunlight into EU. It generates a steady 1 EU/t during daylight hours (6:20 AM to 5:45 PM) in the Overworld, producing approximately 13,050 EU per full day.

The generator requires a clear view of the sky with no opaque blocks above it, and will not generate power during rain, thunderstorms, or at night. It is also dimension-locked to the Overworld.

## Output

- **EU Output**: 1 EU/t (daytime only)
- **Energy Storage**: 400 EU
- **Tier**: 1
- **Requires**: clear sky view, daytime, no rain, Overworld only

## Slots

- Battery slot: chargeable battery or electric tool

The Solar Generator does not accept EU input. It outputs EU from every side except its front face.
