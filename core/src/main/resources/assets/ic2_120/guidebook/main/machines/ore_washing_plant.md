# Ore Washing Plant

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:ore_washing_plant" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Ore Washing Plant washes crushed ores to produce purified crushed ores, along with secondary byproducts and stone dust. It requires a water supply to operate and forms the second step in the IC2 ore processing chain, after the Macerator.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 16,000 EU
- **Tier:** 1
- **Energy Usage:** ~80 EU/t per operation
- **Fluid:** consumes Water (mB) per operation

## Slots

- Top left slot: input (crushed ore)
- Top right slot: output (purified crushed ore)
- Bottom left slot: secondary output
- Bottom right slot: stone dust output
