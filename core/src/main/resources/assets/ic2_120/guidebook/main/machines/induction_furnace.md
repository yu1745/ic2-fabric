# Induction Furnace

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:induction_furnace" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Induction Furnace is an advanced electric furnace that requires preheating before it can smelt items. It uses heat as an intermediate step and offers faster processing at higher temperatures. The furnace must reach 1,000 heat before it begins operating.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 1,600 EU
- **Tier:** 2
- **Heating Energy:** up to 32 EU/t (heat rises until 1,000)
- **Smelting Speed:** varies with heat level

## Slots

- 2 left slots: input
- 2 right slots: output
- Top slot: overclocker upgrades (heaters)
- Bottom slot: transformer upgrades
- Right slot: chargeable battery or electric tool
