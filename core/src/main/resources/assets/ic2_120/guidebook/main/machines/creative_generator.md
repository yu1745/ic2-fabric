# Creative Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:creative_generator" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Creative Generator produces an unlimited supply of EU for testing and creative builds. It generates 512 EU/t directly with no fuel requirement and outputs from all six sides.

This block is only available in creative mode and is intended for machine testing, power grid validation, and builds that demand infinite energy.

## Output

- **EU Output**: 512 EU/t (infinite source)
- **Storage**: None (pass-through)
- **Tier**: 1
- **Creative only**: not obtainable in survival

## Recipe

*Not obtainable in survival mode.*
