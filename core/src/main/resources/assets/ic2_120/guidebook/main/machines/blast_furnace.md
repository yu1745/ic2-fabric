# Blast Furnace

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:blast_furnace" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Blast Furnace uses heat (HU) from the IC2 heat network to smelt materials at high temperatures. Its primary use is producing steel from a combination of iron and coal dust.

## Output

- **Energy Source:** Heat (HU)
- **Primary Recipe:** Iron Dust + Coal Dust → Steel

## Slots

- Top left: fluid input
- Top right: fluid output
- Bottom row: item input/output
