# Steel Transmission Shaft

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:steel_transmission_shaft" scale="4" />

The Steel Transmission Shaft transmits kinetic power (KU) in a straight line. It is a high-tier shaft capable of handling significant kinetic loads.

## Throughput

The Steel Transmission Shaft can handle up to **512 KU** of kinetic power.

## Usage

- Place shafts in a line to transmit kinetic power from a kinetic generator to a kinetic machine.
- Power is transmitted from one shaft to the next when placed adjacent.
- Steel shafts connect on all six faces.
- Steel shafts are required for medium-to-high-power kinetic systems such as those powered by steam kinetic generators.
