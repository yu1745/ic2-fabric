# Carbon Rotor

The Carbon Rotor is the best-tier rotor used in Wind Kinetic Generators and Water Kinetic Generators.

- **Wind**: Radius 5, Multiplier 4&times;
- **Water**: Radius 2.5, Multiplier 4&times;

It provides the maximum possible performance with the largest radius and highest power multiplier available for kinetic generators.
