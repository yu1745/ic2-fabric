# Stirling Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:stirling_generator" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Stirling Generator converts heat (HU) into EU. It must be placed adjacent to a heat source, which transfers heat into the generator. The generator converts 2 HU into 1 EU, with a maximum output of 50 EU/t.

This machine is a Tier 2 device and provides a substantial energy buffer, making it suitable for converting heat from solid fuel heaters, fluid fuel fireboxes, or electric heat generators into usable electricity.

## Output

- **EU Output**: Up to 50 EU/t
- **Heat Conversion**: 2 HU = 1 EU
- **Energy Storage**: 30,000 EU
- **Max Heat Input**: 100 HU/t
- **Tier**: 2

## Slots

The Stirling Generator does not have item slots. It receives heat through its heat transfer face from an adjacent heat source.

The Stirling Generator does not accept EU input. It outputs EU from every side except its front face.
