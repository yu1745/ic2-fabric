# Crop Harvester

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:crop_harvester" p:facing="north" scale="4" />

The Crop Harvester automatically harvests fully grown IC2 crops within a **3x3x3** area centered on the machine, collecting drops and leaving the crop planted for regrowth.

## Energy and Storage

- **EU Storage**: 10,000 EU
- **Tier**: 1 (LV, 32 EU/packet)

## Operation

The Crop Harvester scans its 3x3x3 range for mature IC2 crops. When a mature crop is found, it harvests the crop, collecting all drops into its output inventory. The crop plant remains in place and can regrow without needing to be replanted.

## Usage

Position the Crop Harvester at the center of your crop farm. Provide EU power and ensure the output slots have space for harvested drops. The harvester will automatically collect mature crops, greatly reducing manual labor for large-scale farming operations.
