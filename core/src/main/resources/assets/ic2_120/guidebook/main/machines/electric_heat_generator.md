# Electric Heat Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:electric_heat_generator" scale="4" />

The Electric Heat Generator consumes EU to produce heat (HU). It is the most convenient heat source when an EU grid is already available, converting electrical power into heat with good efficiency.

## Output

The Electric Heat Generator has an internal EU buffer of **10,000 EU** and produces up to **10 HU/t** when supplied with EU. The conversion rate is 2 EU per 1 HU.

## Slots

- Top slot: chargeable battery or energy crystal
- Bottom slot: (not used)

## Usage

Connect an EU source (generator, cable, or energy storage) to any face. The machine will convert EU into heat and output HU from all faces. It can charge batteries placed in the top slot as well, but its primary function is EU-to-HU conversion.
