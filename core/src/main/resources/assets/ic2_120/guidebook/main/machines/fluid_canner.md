# Fluid Canner

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:fluid_canner" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Fluid Canner fills fluid containers (cells, cans) with liquids or empties them. It can both fill empty cells with fluid from its internal tank or drain filled cells into the tank.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 250 EU
- **Tier:** 1

## Slots

- Left slot: fluid container input (empty or filled)
- Right slot: fluid container output (filled or empty)
