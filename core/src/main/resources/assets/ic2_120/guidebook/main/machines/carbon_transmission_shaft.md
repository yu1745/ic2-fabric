# Carbon Fiber Transmission Shaft

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:carbon_transmission_shaft" scale="4" />

The Carbon Fiber Transmission Shaft transmits kinetic power (KU) in a straight line. It is the highest-tier shaft, capable of handling extreme kinetic loads.

## Throughput

The Carbon Fiber Transmission Shaft can handle up to **2,048 KU** of kinetic power.

## Usage

- Place shafts in a line to transmit kinetic power from a kinetic generator to a kinetic machine.
- Power is transmitted from one shaft to the next when placed adjacent.
- Carbon shafts connect on all six faces.
- Carbon fiber shafts are required for the most demanding kinetic systems and can handle the output of any kinetic generator without loss.
