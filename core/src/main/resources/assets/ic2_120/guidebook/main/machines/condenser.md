# Condenser

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:condenser" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Condenser converts steam into distilled water by cooling it. It accepts EU input and vents excess energy when its internal buffer is full. It is part of the IC2 heat-based processing chain.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 100,000 EU
- **Tier:** 3
- **Conversion:** Steam → Distilled Water
- **Excess:** Vents surplus EU when full
