# Animal Slaughterer

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:animal_slaughterer" p:facing="north" scale="4" />

The Animal Slaughterer automatically butchers fully grown adult animals within a **3x3x3** area centered on the machine, collecting meat, leather, wool, and other animal drops.

## Energy and Storage

- **EU Storage**: 10,000 EU
- **Tier**: 1 (LV, 32 EU/packet)

## Operation

The Animal Slaughterer scans its 3x3x3 range for adult animals. When a mature animal is detected, it humanely butchers the animal and collects the drops into its output inventory. Young animals are left alone to grow.

## Usage

Place the Animal Slaughterer adjacent to your animal pens. Supply EU power and ensure the output inventory has space. Combine with an Animal-Matron for a fully automated breeding-and-butchering loop: the matron breeds animals and the slaughterer harvests them once they reach adulthood.
