# Kinetic Generator

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:kinetic_generator" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Kinetic Generator converts kinetic energy (KU) from the kinetic transmission network into EU. It pulls KU from adjacent transmission shafts, gears, or kinetic generators connected to its front face and converts it at a rate of 4 KU = 1 EU.

This machine is the primary interface between the kinetic and electrical power systems. It is a Tier 3 device capable of handling up to 2,048 KU/t input and converting it to up to 512 EU/t output.

## Output

- **Conversion Rate**: 4 KU = 1 EU
- **Max KU Input**: 2,048 KU/t
- **Max EU Output**: 512 EU/t
- **Energy Storage**: 10,000 EU
- **Tier**: 3

## Slots

The Kinetic Generator does not have item slots. It accepts KU through its front face from the kinetic network.

The Kinetic Generator outputs EU from every side except its front face.
