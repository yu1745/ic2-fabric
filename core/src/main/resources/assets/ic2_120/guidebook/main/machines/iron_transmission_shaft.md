# Iron Transmission Shaft

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:iron_transmission_shaft" scale="4" />

The Iron Transmission Shaft transmits kinetic power (KU) in a straight line. It is a mid-tier shaft that handles more power than wood but less than steel or carbon.

## Throughput

The Iron Transmission Shaft can handle up to **128 KU** of kinetic power.

## Usage

- Place shafts in a line to transmit kinetic power from a kinetic generator to a kinetic machine.
- Power is transmitted from one shaft to the next when placed adjacent.
- Iron shafts connect on all six faces.
- Iron shafts are more durable than wood shafts and suitable for general-purpose kinetic systems.
