# Animal-Matron

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:animalmatron" p:facing="north" scale="4" />

The Animal-Matron is an automated livestock management machine that feeds and breeds animals within a **3x3x3** area centered on the machine.

## Energy and Storage

- **EU Storage**: 10,000 EU
- **Water Tank**: 8 buckets

## Functions

- **Feeding**: Automatically feeds animals in range using food items from its input slots.
- **Breeding**: When animals are fed, they enter love mode and breed, producing offspring automatically.
- **Water**: Uses water for certain feeding operations.

## Usage

Place the Animal-Matron near your animal pens. Supply appropriate animal food (wheat for cows, carrots for pigs, seeds for chickens, etc.) into the input slots. Keep the machine powered with EU and ensure it has water in its tank. The matron will handle feeding and breeding automatically within its range.
