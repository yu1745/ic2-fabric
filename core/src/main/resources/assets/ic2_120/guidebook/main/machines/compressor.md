# Compressor

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:compressor" p:facing="north" p:active="true" scale="4" />

The Compressor compresses items and materials into more compact forms, often used to create advanced materials such as carbon plates, diamonds from coal blocks, and other compressed items.

## Output

- **EU Storage:** 600 EU
- **Tier:** 1
- **Energy Usage:** ~12 EU/t per operation

## Slots

- Top left slot: input
- Top right slot: output
