# Composite (Alloy) Chestplate

<ItemImage id="ic2_120:alloy_chestplate" scale="4" />

The Composite Chestplate (also called the Alloy Chestplate) is a single-slot, high-protection chest piece. It does not need to be worn as a set — there are no other "Composite" pieces — and it sits in the chestplate slot by itself, making it the natural choice when you do not want a [BatPack / Energy Pack / LapPack](../reference/energy_items.md) or a [Jetpack](../reference/energy_items.md) in that slot.

## Stats

| Slot | Item | Armor | Durability Multiplier | Repair Ingredient |
|------|------|:---:|:---:|---|
| Chestplate | alloy_chestplate | 9 | 33x | <ItemLink id="ic2_120:advanced_alloy" /> |

The 9 armor points match a diamond chestplate (528 vanilla durability equivalent), and the 33x multiplier pushes the effective durability far past diamond or netherite. It is the single most protective chest piece the mod offers on raw stats alone.

## How to Use

- Wear the Composite Chestplate in the chestplate slot. There are no other Composite pieces — the helmet, leggings, and boots slots are free for whatever you want.
- This makes the Composite Chestplate a popular pairing with [Nano Armor](nano_armor.md) or [Quantum Armor](quantum_armor.md) pieces: a Quantum Helmet plus a Composite Chestplate plus Quantum Leggings plus Quantum Boots gives you the Quantum set's energy mechanics on every slot except the chest, while still leaving 9 armor points on the chest.
- Repair with <ItemLink id="ic2_120:advanced_alloy" /> in an anvil or crafting grid. The advanced alloy is itself crafted from <ItemLink id="ic2_120:mixed_metal_ingot" /> and a small amount of <ItemLink id="ic2_120:bronze_ingot" />, so a steady repair supply is reasonable once the player has an Induction Furnace.
- The chestplate is **non-powered**. It does not store EU, does not consume EU, and does not run any of the Quantum / Nano energy mechanics. It is pure armor.

## Why a Single-Slot Chestplate

Most IC2 chestplate-slot items come with a tradeoff: a BatPack, Advanced BatPack, Energy Pack, LapPack, Jetpack, or Electric Jetpack gives you power or flight at the cost of an armor slot. The Composite Chestplate exists for the opposite case: pure tank, no power. Pair it with energy armor pieces in the other three slots and you get the best of both worlds.
