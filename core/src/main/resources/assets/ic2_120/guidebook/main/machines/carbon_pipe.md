# Carbon Pipe

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:carbon_pipe_tiny" scale="4" />

Carbon pipes are advanced fluid transport pipes with significantly higher throughput than bronze pipes. They are crafted from carbon fiber and are ideal for high-volume fluid systems.

## Size Comparison

| Size | Throughput | Use Case |
|------|------------|----------|
| Tiny | 1 B/t (1,000 mB/t max) | Low-volume precision transfer |
| Small | 4 B/t (4,000 mB/t max) | General purpose |
| Medium | 8 B/t (8,000 mB/t max) | High-volume transfer |
| Large | 16 B/t (16,000 mB/t max) | Bulk fluid transport |

## Usage

- Pipes connect to adjacent fluid tanks, machines, and other pipes automatically.
- Fluids flow through connected pipes from a source to a destination.
- A Pump Attachment is required to extract fluids from tanks and machines automatically.
- Right-click the pipe with a wrench to configure connection sides.
- Carbon pipes are more durable than bronze pipes and resistant to higher temperatures.
