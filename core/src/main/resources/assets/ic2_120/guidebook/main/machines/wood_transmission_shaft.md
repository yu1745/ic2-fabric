# Wood Transmission Shaft

<BlockImage id="ic2_120:wood_transmission_shaft" scale="4" />

The Wood Transmission Shaft transmits kinetic power (KU) in a straight line. It is the simplest and lowest-tier shaft, suitable for low-power kinetic systems.

## Throughput

The Wood Transmission Shaft can handle up to **32 KU** of kinetic power.

## Usage

- Place shafts in a line to transmit kinetic power from a kinetic generator to a kinetic machine.
- Power is transmitted from one shaft to the next when placed adjacent.
- Wood shafts connect on all six faces.
- Higher-tier shafts (Iron, Steel, Carbon) can handle more KU and are needed for demanding applications.
