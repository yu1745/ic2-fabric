# Bronze Armor

<ItemImage id="ic2_120:bronze_helmet" scale="4" />

The Bronze Armor set is IC2's baseline four-piece protection. It is the first metal-tier armor the player will likely craft, sitting roughly between vanilla leather and iron. Bronze is cheap to make, repairs with <ItemLink id="ic2_120:bronze_ingot" />, and offers the same `8x` durability multiplier across all four slots.

Durability values: Helmet 88, Chestplate 128, Leggings 120, Boots 104 (vanilla base `x 8`).

## How to Use

- Wear all four pieces for the full armor set bonus, or mix individual pieces with other armor.
- Bronze is a soft metal — once you have iron or better, switch over. Bronze is a stepping stone, not an end-game set.
- Combine the chestplate with <ItemLink id="ic2_120:bronze_ingot" /> in an anvil or crafting grid to repair.
- All four pieces also have **knockback resistance 0.0** and **toughness 0.0** — purely baseline protection.
