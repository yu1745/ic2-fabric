# Crop Items and Drinks

Beyond the crops themselves, the crop system also covers Seed Bags, scanning, weeding, and the drink chain.

## Crop Tools

- **Crop Stick**: placed on tilled farmland as the basis for IC2 crops.
- **Crop Seed Bag**: stores a crop's type, growth, gain, resistance, and other attributes.
- **Cropnalyzer**: analyzes a Seed Bag's attributes.
- **Weeding Spade**: clears weeds off a Crop Stick.
- **Weed-EX**: consumes its internal supply to suppress weeds.
- **Fertilizer**: improves the crop's care efficiency.

## Harvests

Crops can yield Hops, Coffee Beans, Terra Wart, Weeds, Toad Powder, Flour, and more. For per-crop output see [Crop Harvest Output Reference](crop_harvest.md).

## Drinks

- The Stone Mug holds drinks.
- Coffee Beans can be processed into Coffee Powder, and then brewed into Coffee, Cold Coffee, or Dark Coffee.
- The crop drink chain pairs well with automated farms and processing machines.
